Compile Bootstrap with your own asset pipeline by downloading our source Sass, JavaScript, and documentation files. This option requires some additional tooling: Sass compiler (Libsass or Ruby Sass is supported) for compiling your CSS. Autoprefixer for CSS vendor prefixing Should you require build tools, they are included for developing Bootstrap and its docs, but they’re likely unsuitable for your own purposes.
